Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AmazeD ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our split-screen multiplayer game and its companion smartphone web controller.

1. Information We Collect

AmazeD is designed to be a local-network focused multiplayer experience. We collect the bare minimum information necessary to make the game function smoothly:

  • Connection Data: IP addresses and short connection codes are used strictly for pairing your smartphone controller with the main game client.
  • Gameplay Data: In-game actions, character selections, and movement inputs are transmitted in real-time to manage the match.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of operating the game. Specifically, the data acts as the bridge allowing real-time communication between your smartphone (acting as a controller/minimap) and the main game screen.

3. Data Sharing and Storage

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Connection and gameplay data are processed in real-time and are temporarily held in memory. We do not store your gameplay data or personal identifiers on external servers once your session ends.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at: support@amazed.com
