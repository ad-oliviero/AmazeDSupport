About the Game

AmazeD is an epic split-screen multiplayer labyrinth game where players use their smartphones as controllers. Navigate procedurally generated mazes, avoid obstacles, and compete against your friends in real-time!

Features

  • Phone as a Controller: Connect your smartphone to the game using a simple code. Your phone displays a dynamic minimap and character-specific UI.
  • Unique Characters: Choose from 6 distinct heroes (Myra, Zed, Flick, Otnas, Nixie, Yumi), each with their own unique color theme.
  • Dynamic Mazes: Every match features a newly generated labyrinth so you never play the same game twice.

Support & FAQ

Q: How do I connect my phone to play?
A: Launch the game on your main screen (PC/Console) and wait for the lobby to generate a Connection Code. Open this website on your smartphone, enter the code, and select your character!
Q: My phone is disconnecting during the match.
A: Ensure that both your main device and your smartphone are connected to a stable Wi-Fi network. Avoid locking your phone screen while playing.
