FAQ

How do I connect my phone as a controller?

Make sure your phone and your PC/Mac/iPad are on the same Wi-Fi network. Open the game, and scan the QR code or enter the IP address on your phone browser.

How many players can play?

AmazeD supports up to 4 players in local split-screen multiplayer.

Does it require an internet connection?

No, the game runs locally on your Wi-Fi network (LAN). No internet connection is strictly required to play with friends at home.
