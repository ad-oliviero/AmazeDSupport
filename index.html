About the Game

An epic local multiplayer labyrinth game powered by your smartphone.

Phone as a Controller

Connect your smartphone using a simple code to view your dynamic minimap and character UI.

Unique Characters

Choose from 6 distinct heroes (Myra, Zed, Flick, Otnas, Nixie, Yumi), each with their own color theme.

Dynamic Mazes

How to Play

A 360-degree overview of the AmazeD experience.

1. Connect

Join the lobby using the connection code. Your phone instantly transforms into your personal game controller with a dynamic minimap.

2. Explore

Navigate the maze while keeping an eye on the main split-screen display. Watch out for dead ends and race against your friends.

3. Survive the Hurry Up

When the clock runs down, the music shifts into high gear and the Exit Portal spawns directly inside the maze walls. Be the first to escape!

FAQ

How do I connect my phone to play?

Launch the game on your main screen (PC/Console) and wait for the lobby to generate a Connection Code. Open this website on your smartphone, enter the code, and select your character!

My phone is disconnecting during the match.

Ensure that both your main device and your smartphone are connected to a stable Wi-Fi network. Avoid locking your phone screen while playing.
